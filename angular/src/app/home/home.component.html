Signals vs RxJS Decision

  • RxJS is used for search input because it handles asynchronous streams, cancellation, debouncing, and errors well.
  • Signals are used for cart and order UI state because they provide simple, fine-grained reactivity.
  • Computed signals update derived values like total quantity and total price without manually subscribing or recalculating in components.
